## High-Risk Areas Requiring Special Attention

### Financial Services
- **Disclaimers:** Ensure "not financial advice" disclaimers are prominent and enforceable
- **Accuracy:** Liability for incorrect ISA allowance calculations
- **FCA Rules:** Determine if any features trigger FCA oversight

### Data Protection
- **Local Storage Claims:** If you claim data is local-only, this MUST be technically accurate
- **International Transfers:** If you add server storage or use US-based analytics, additional compliance needed
- **Special Category Data:** Financial data may require extra protections

### Liability Limitations
- **Consumer Rights:** Cannot exclude liability for negligence, fraud, or death/injury under UK law
- **Unfair Terms:** Consumer Rights Act 2015 prohibits unfair contract terms
- **Financial Loss:** Liability caps must be reasonable and enforceable
